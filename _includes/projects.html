ReeFood

A web app that keeps track of the food stored in your fridge with expiry dates.
It will notify you if any food item is approaching its expiry date.
Thus, helping in reducing food wastage.
It also recommends food recipes that can be prepared using the items in the fridge so that you don't have to buy any extra food item that may lead to more food wastage.

Tech Stack - React.js, Node.js, Firebase, Nodemailer
